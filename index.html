The problem

The Nordic market went flow-based. The data didn't get any friendlier.

The signal that moves Nordic spreads is buried in thousands of constraint rows an hour — and the people who can read it sell it back to you behind a sales call.

Raw & awkward

Flow-based congestion is published as thousands of constraint rows per hour. Useful in theory, unreadable in practice without grid-topology context.

Spreads, not zones

Congestion rent and hedge basis live in the spread between zones. Most tools still hand you zonal prices and leave the math to you.

Gated & expensive

The best Nordic analytics sit behind enterprise contracts and demos. There is no clean, public front door you can just open and read.

What Kraftatlas is

Every dataset on one stage — plus the forecast.

A free, beautiful live map as the front door, with congestion tools you can use today and a spread-risk forecast on the way — the whole Nordic picture in one place.
