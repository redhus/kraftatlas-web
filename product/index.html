03 · The spread forecast Record accruing

Forecast the spread you actually trade — scored in the open.

A calibrated model publishes a probabilistic spread-risk forecast before gate closure, every morning — and every call lands on a dated, immutable record. The primary scored product is monthly EPAD fair value, SE3 · SE4 · FI first; the public scoreboard starts 1 Aug 2026.

Quantile fans

Not a single number — a calibrated distribution. Median plus the spread of plausible outcomes for tomorrow.

Tail probabilities

The numbers a desk hedges on: P(spread < −10), P(|spread| > 20). Probabilities, checked against what actually happened.

Named drivers

Not just "risky day" — which constraint is driving it and how active it's been. Alerts you can argue with, named down to the cut.
